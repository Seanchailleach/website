Greetings from the Weather Witch!


Why “weather witch?”

Most people don’t understand what a “witch” really is. No, they don’t fly around on broomsticks, turning people into toads (believe me, if that were possible, I’d be all over it; I already have a Toad List). A witch is basically anyone—male, female, or otherwise—who lives life in tune with nature and the cycles of the seasons, and who gathers wisdom from the natural world. Witches were/are herbalists, gardeners/farmers, midwives, healers of all sorts, the elders of their communities—and yes, weather watchers!

My intent for this site is to bring together the wisdom of the old ways and modern technology, with emphasis on weather watching and forecasting. Why? Because I’m a weather nerd. I’m as enthralled with radar and modern weather forecasting models as I am with reading the signs of nature to predict the weather. I don’t think it’s an either/or; “both” is perfectly reasonable.

Not gonna lie, my HTML skills are from the 1990s so this is going to be a long-term and tedious (at least for me) project. Expect numerous, and hopefully frequent, updates. I promise pics and prettiness and witchy nature stuff in the future! But for now, I plan to plug along a bit every day.
